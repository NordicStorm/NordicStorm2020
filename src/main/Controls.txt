Controls:
    Right Stick:
        Forward/Twist: driving
        Throttle: drive throttle
        Trigger (hold): follow ball
        Right thumb button (btn 4): shift
        Button 9: arc right
        Button 11: arc left
    Left Stick:
        Trigger (hold): shoot
        Left thumb button (btn 4): manually run intake
        Throttle: control the arc radius for the driver
